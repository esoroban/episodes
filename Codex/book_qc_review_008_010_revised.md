## Episode 8 — `Третья причина`

### What works
- Strong dramatic trigger: number drops without the Mirror.
- The metaphor `кукловод` is memorable and child-readable.
- The lesson is well fused with the plot: the concept is discovered through the actual mystery.

### Critique
- The vase example is not fully closed as written.
  - Current:
    - `Ты пришёл — и ваза упала! Это из-за тебя!`
  - Problem:
    - the child does not know whether the person actually touched the vase, table, or floor.
    - the keyed answer `ловушка` depends on hidden context not present in the question.
  - Reference:
    - [book/ep_008.md](/Users/iuriinovosolov/Documents/SylaSlovaDramma/book/ep_008.md:137)

- The cartoons/late sleep example is also under-specified.
  - Current:
    - `Ребёнок смотрит мультики допоздна И ложится поздно.`
  - Problem:
    - this can honestly read as direct cause, not only third cause.
  - Reference:
    - [book/ep_008.md](/Users/iuriinovosolov/Documents/SylaSlovaDramma/book/ep_008.md:157)

- The final inference about the Mirror needs tighter wording.
  - Current dramatic reading risks sliding from:
    - `артефакт не единственная причина`
  - into:
    - `артефакт вообще не причина`
  - But your story has already shown a real cost of Mirror use.
  - Reference:
    - [book/ep_008.md](/Users/iuriinovosolov/Documents/SylaSlovaDramma/book/ep_008.md:171)

### Improvements
- Rewrite hidden-context cases so the missing fact is stated inside the question.
- Prefer formulations of the type:
  - `Даже если убрать X, Y всё равно случается. X — единственная причина?`
- Tighten the conclusion:
  - `Зеркало — не единственная причина`
  - not
  - `Зеркало не причина`
